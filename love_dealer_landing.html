Meet Jamie Levine

Jamie has spent over a decade building relationships inside the Jewish community — as a connector, an advisor, and now as a professional matchmaker. She founded The Love Dealer because she believed the Jewish dating world deserved something more personal than an app.

Her approach is direct, warm, and deeply knowledgeable about the nuances that matter: observance levels, family dynamics, cultural backgrounds, what "serious" actually means to different people.

She doesn't just find you a date. She finds you a partner.
